About Us

Meet the family

Originated by the family of Glen and Jean Austin, the farm has been providing Norfolk with pre-picked and pick-your-own produce for years.

The farm was sold to Mark and Maureen Sloot, nee Austin, who have been operating it with their three children since 1995. The family specialized in strawberries but expanded into different crops to meet the substantial requests of their customers. The farm is conveniently located south of Boston, Ontario on Cockshutt Road, and is simply minutes from Hamilton, highway 403 and Port Dover.

We take pride in maintaining locally grown and produced fruits and vegetables for our farm market that you can buy with peace of mind.

Market

Baked goods and local produce

Experience all of the benefits of buying fresh, local produce when you visit our farm market. Not only will you get great value, but you will help to strengthen communities, support local families and invest in the future.

Canned and baked goods are made from scratch right here on the farm, using all local products. Enjoy a selection of jams, jellies, pickled veggies, and sauces. Craving a sweet treat? We have fresh baked pies, cakes, bread and various desserts throughout the market.
