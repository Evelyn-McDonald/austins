Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will Nazca guarantee profits, or give financial advice?

    Nazca is a simple to use yet advanced tool to help you create and manage your investment strategies, however it does not give investment advice. You should choose to invest on your own terms, and within assets \ projects you understand. Markets are very volatile, which does offer opportunities for both short and long-term investment, but either way, no substantial return is guaranteed. If you do decide to invest, make sure you do have researched and that you’re confident in what you are doing.

  • Why am I required to add API keys? Is it safe? Where are my funds stored?

    Nazca never holds your funds, but makes all the trades for you through the exchange’s API keys: your holdings remain stored on your exchange account, and managed with trade-only APIs. Your keys are encrypted and the connection is secured.

  • Is there a minimum balance required to run a strategy?

    Although there is not a minimum balance required, you should take into account the exchange’s minimum trading limit per coin, fees, and your model, to make sure your strategy can be properly executed.

  • What about taxes?

    Nazca reduces the number of taxable events to a minimum, but depending on the regulations in your country, you might still want to pay close attention to the effect rebalancing will have on your profits. Make sure you pick and appropriate rebalancing interval. List of all trades will be available for download in your exchange account.

  • Should I hold a specific coin in my account to start with?

    You can start with any currency; when you execute your strategy, Nazca will sell everything that is needed to purchase the assets that reflect your strategy, in the most efficient way.

  • Do you offer a referral program? How does it work?

    Yes, we do! For every three paying users you refer, you earn a free month. You will find your referral code and updated count in your profile, from where you can also redeem the free month when eligible.

  • How would Nazca behave if the exchange is in maintenance or having issues, and the rebalancing can’t be executed at the scheduled time?

    Nazca will retry five times (at longer intervals every time) until execution succeeds, or fails. If the exchange has been offline for too much time and it fails, you can manually rebalance from your Dashboard. The next rebalance will always happen at the define time.
    Example: even if the exchange was down and the rebalance happens 5 hours later than it should have, the next one will not have a 5 hour delay.

  • Will the payment be automatic/subscription?

    Unfortunately, Coinbase (or any form of cryptocurrency payments) does not support subscriptions or recurring payments, so you will be required to purchase manually. However, we will send you a reminder via email when the next payment is due, so you won't miss out on next rebalance.

  • What happens to my strategy if I don’t pay for the next period due? Will my account be deleted?

    Absolutely not! Your account and dashboard will be fully accessible, and if you had a strategy active, it will be paused until you renew.
